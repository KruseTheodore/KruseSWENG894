Info


This application is designed to allow users to communicate about bourbons and get reviews from a large group of individuals.
This will help potential buyers of bourbon make educated purchases and more often aquire bourbon that they enjoy. As the
developer of this application I have felt the desire for this sort of information many times as I am looking at the prices
of bourbons on the shelves. My hope is that this application can help users aquire information that gives them more comfort
when they are making bourbon purchases themselves.
